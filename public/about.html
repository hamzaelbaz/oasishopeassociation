Our Mission

For the Oasis, For the Future

At Oasis Hope Association, our mission is to restore and protect the oases of southern Morocco through reforestation, environmental awareness, and cultural exchange. We work alongside local communities and international volunteers to revive fragile ecosystems, promote sustainable practices, and safeguard these unique landscapes for generations to come.

What is Oasis Hope Association?

We are a non-profit focused on protecting and restoring the oases of southern Morocco through reforestation, education, and sustainable projects.

How can I help?

You can support us by volunteering, donating, or sharing our mission with others.

Are your activities eco-friendly?

Yes. We focus on planting native trees, conserving water, and promoting sustainable practices.

Can I volunteer?

Yes, we welcome volunteers! Please visit our Volunteer page to see possibilities and join up for events and activities that align with your interests and availability.

Who benefits from your work?

Local farmers, children, and communities in southern Morocco, as well as the fragile oasis ecosystems.
